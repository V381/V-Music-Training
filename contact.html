About Our Mission

Music Learning Tools is dedicated to making music education accessible to everyone. Our tools are free, open-source, and continuously improving thanks to community feedback and contributions. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced musician, we're here to support your musical journey.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are these tools really free?

Yes, all our tools are completely free and open-source. We believe in making music education accessible to everyone.

How can I contribute?

You can contribute by submitting pull requests on GitHub, reporting issues, or suggesting improvements via email.

Do I need to create an account?

No account is required to use our tools. Simply visit the tool you want to use and start practicing!
